How this version works

Enter D1 and D9 manually. The app validates the charts, sends them to the Cloudflare function, then returns rule-based domain insights for identity, wealth, marriage, career, EMA risk, and health.

  • Enter planets house-wise, comma separated: Sun, Moon, Mars, Mercury, Jupiter, Venus, Saturn, Rahu, Ketu.
  • Each planet should appear once in D1 and once in D9.
